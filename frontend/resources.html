Safety and Preventive Measures

Safety Tips

- Keep emergency contacts easily accessible.
- Inform trusted individuals about your location.
- Plan safe exit strategies in case of danger.

Preventive Measures

- Promote open communication in relationships.
- Participate in GBV awareness programs.
- Encourage bystander intervention in unsafe situations.
